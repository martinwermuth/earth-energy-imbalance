# Earth's Energy Imbalance - NASA CERES Visualization

**Live:** https://martinwermuth.github.io/earth-energy-imbalance/

**[EN]** Interactive, bilingual (EN/DE) visualization of NASA CERES satellite measurements of Earth's energy budget - absorbed sunlight vs. heat radiated to space, March 2000 through April 2026. Two standalone HTML files with embedded data and Chart.js. No external dependencies, no tracking - runs in any browser, also offline.

**[DE]** Interaktive, zweisprachige Visualisierung der NASA-CERES-Satellitenmessungen zur Energiebilanz der Erde - absorbiertes Sonnenlicht gegen abgestrahlte Waerme, Maerz 2000 bis April 2026. Zwei eigenstaendige HTML-Dateien mit eingebetteten Daten und Chart.js. Keine externen Abhaengigkeiten, kein Tracking - laeuft in jedem Browser, auch offline.

## Pages / Seiten

- **[index.html](https://martinwermuth.github.io/earth-energy-imbalance/)** - Global: widening gap, imbalance magnitude, ASR/OLR anomalies, albedo, cumulative energy, paleo comparison. Unit toggle: W/m2, Hiroshima bombs, zettajoules. CSV export, copy-chart, share box.
- **[regions.html](https://martinwermuth.github.io/earth-energy-imbalance/regions.html)** - Regional: North Atlantic, North Pacific, South Pacific, Nino 3.4, hemispheres, Arctic, tropics. Six views incl. IMO 2020 comparison. Ocean-masked where appropriate.

## Data / Daten

NASA CERES EBAF-TOA Edition 4.2.1, monthly means, 2000-03 to 2026-04. All regional values are calibrated against the official global NASA time series (calibration period Jul 2005 - Jun 2015: 0.74 W/m2; calendar year 2023: 1.84 W/m2).

**Citation:** NASA/LARC/SD/ASDC. CERES Energy Balanced and Filled (EBAF) TOA Monthly Means, Edition 4.2.1. DOI: [10.5067/TERRA-AQUA-NOAA20/CERES/EBAF-TOA_L3B004.2.1](https://doi.org/10.5067/TERRA-AQUA-NOAA20/CERES/EBAF-TOA_L3B004.2.1) - Loeb et al. (2018), J. Climate 31, 895-918.

## Updates

Data is updated manually each quarter via a new subset order from the NASA Langley ASDC CERES ordering tool. Current last data month: **2026-04**.
